About

While meeting a friend at his office, the founder saw various inefficiencies such as Orders coming through WhatsApp, lengthy price negotiations on phone and thus, significant valuable time was being spent on those matters where technology simply could help. Simply originated on this concept to help small to midsize business owners to run the business efficiently and systematically with making sure the right person is doing the right thing at the right time.
