HighLights

Get your Wholesale Business Online with ease

24/7 Ordering

Accept Orders anytime, anywhere

When we want to buy something from ecommerce websites like Amazon, Flipkart etc, we just go to the website and order, then why not in Wholesale business? Simply enables your retail customers to place orders 24x7 anytime anywhere irrespective of any exceptions. Get away from old way of accepting orders through phone or whatsapp, adapt this innovative solution and get orders simply. Simply has built in ecosystem to have your agents/customers to place orders anytime anywhere within few minutes.


Order Processing

Track Orders, Bales, and Delivery Notes all at one place

When orders received we need to process the order in terms of packing, creating invoices and shipping the orders. Simply brings one solution to make order processing simple. Get away from old way of doing business with manual process, automate your business using Simply. Have your staff use the system and create transparency in the environment. 0% Error Prone, productivity increases. Track Orders, Bales, and Delivery Notes all in one place customized for Textile industry. Increase the speed of your order delivery


Inventory

Keep track of your inventory, stock. No more customer frustrations over order cancellation due to insufficient stock

By having a inventory management system for you it saves lot of money and time. When received orders, auto update of stock happens so that you are absolutely worriless. Auto updation of stock has multiple advantages like avoids dead stock, better forecasting of future stock requirements, lowers the storage costs, eases in accounting and avoids product spoilage (expiry dates). Simply has all these benefits embraced in managing stock effectively. We hold expertise in textile inventory management system

Visibility

Have complete visibility of incoming orders, order processing, deliveries whether you are at office or out of town

If you would like to build an high productive business, only right things should be visible to the right persons. Simply has an creative solution to have roles in place so that the respective people assigned with roles will have those functionalities only visible. Have complete visibility of incoming orders, order processing, deliveries whether you are at office or out of town. Enables visibility to agents, sales people and customers. No more calls from agents or customers to ask delivery status, LR number. Define roles and responsibilities for your sales people, agents and employees & run the business systematically

Catalog Share

Share your catalogs instantly

Sharing your catalogs to your customers gives an exposure of your stock and enables to get orders proactively. Simply has a catalog sharing feature which not only helps to share catalogs to your customers, but also the customers can choose the designs and place orders instantaneously. Get away from managing catalog in dropbox or manually sending images in Whatsapp, and enable your business to have share catalogs. This will also help to expand your business without limitations of showing designs in person and using technology effectively in this digital world.
