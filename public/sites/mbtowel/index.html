About

We believe that Our products speak for themselves and are committed in providing our customers high quality and long lasting products by utilizing the finest materials available. Our purpose is to surpass customer expectations by offering unique product range to complement their lifestyles while maintaining a high level of value and style.

M.B. Towel was established in 1931 as a trading company under the name of Mafatlal Vadilal. It started off as a hosiery trading company and got expanded in the areas of manufacturing and job work. It was spread all across the villages in Gujarat and also at the national level.

Later in 1999, we started towel agency work and our agencies were spread across India. Next, we started manufacturing and production of towels under our own label, M.B. Towel and it is flourishing since 2000.

In 2005, we started our export business and all India wholesale business. Later in 2011, agency work lead to fabric printing business. Today, we are into fabric printing(specially bedding collections), and manufacturing of towels. We have now also started manufacturing under our own label, M.B. Towel.

M.B. Towel is one of the most prominent exporters of Towels. We also design and manufacture different types of Towel. Yarn dyed, Plain dyed, Zero twist and Terry towels are available in both Dobby and Jacquard. Designs vary from hotel towels, beach towels, kitchen towels, face towels to everyday use towels. We offer towels of fine quality, with wide range of colors and perfect sizes.

M.B. Towel has emerged in fabric printing business. We print on various kinds and counts of fabrics for the bedding collections. The bedding collection comes as a set of a bed sheet, pillows and a comforter. The fabric range varies from cotton, twill, satin, procion and others. We have initiated to design and manufacture bedding collections under our own label, M.B. Towel. Sizes of the product varies from double bed sheet king sized to single bedding.
